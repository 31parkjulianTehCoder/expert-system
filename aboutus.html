About Us

We are team Advanced Hand Sanitizer, the creators of Verdant. At a school-wide competition, three of our future members had Cheeto dust all over our fingers after snacking too much. Another one of our future members had hand sanitizer, and after asking him for hand sanitizer, we started bonding over our love of computer science. For the 2026 OCSEF Hackathon, we created Verdant: a game about building a sustainable city.
